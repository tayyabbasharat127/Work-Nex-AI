# Attendance Policy

Attendance check-in and check-out use the configured attendance timezone. Duplicate check-ins or check-outs are blocked. Late status is based on the configured late threshold.

Working hours are calculated from check-in and check-out. Records below the configured half-day threshold can be marked as half-day. Admin manual corrections are organization-scoped and audited.

Managers can view only direct subordinate attendance inside their organization. Employees can view only their own attendance.
