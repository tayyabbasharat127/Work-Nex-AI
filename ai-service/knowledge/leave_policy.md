# Leave Policy

Employees can apply for leave from the My Leaves page. Leave requests are checked against active leave policies, role eligibility, date validity, overlapping pending or approved leave, and available balance.

Managers can approve or reject requests only for direct subordinates in the same organization. Admins can approve organization-scoped requests. Missing or insufficient leave balance blocks a request.

AI may parse policy documents, but parsed policy rules stay inactive until an admin approves them. The deterministic rule engine makes final leave decisions.
