WorkNex AI Demo Leave Policy

This document is a sample HR leave policy for testing the Policy Document Automation upload, extraction, AI parse, and admin approval workflow.

ANNUAL Leave
Annual leave has an annual quota of 18 days per year.
Annual leave is applicable to EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, and ADMIN roles.
Minimum notice is 7 days before the leave start date.
Maximum consecutive 10 days may be taken at one time.
Carry forward is allowed up to 5 days.
Annual leave requires manager approval.

SICK Leave
Sick leave has an annual quota of 10 days per year.
Sick leave is applicable to EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, and ADMIN roles.
Minimum notice is 0 days because sick leave may be urgent.
Maximum consecutive 5 days may be taken at one time.
A medical certificate is required after 2 days.
Sick leave requires manager approval.

CASUAL Leave
Casual leave has an annual quota of 8 days per year.
Casual leave is applicable to EMPLOYEE and MANAGER roles.
Minimum notice is 2 days before the leave start date.
Maximum consecutive 3 days may be taken at one time.
Carry forward is not allowed.
Casual leave requires manager approval.

UNPAID Leave
Unpaid leave has an annual quota of 30 days per year.
Unpaid leave is applicable to EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, and ADMIN roles.
Minimum notice is 14 days before the leave start date.
Maximum consecutive 30 days may be taken at one time.
Unpaid leave requires manager approval and admin approval.

Probation Rule
Leave during probation is not allowed unless HR approval is granted.
