WORKNEX LEAVE POLICY SAMPLE

Instructions:
Replace the sample values below with your organization's rules, then upload this file from Leave Management.
Supported role names are EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN, and SUPER_ADMIN.

ANNUAL LEAVE
Annual quota: 18 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 10 days.
Minimum notice: 7 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Carry forward is allowed up to 5 days.
Manager approval is required.
Probation employees are not allowed to use annual leave.

SICK LEAVE
Annual quota: 10 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 5 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
A medical certificate is required after 2 days.
Manager approval is required.
No carry forward.

CASUAL LEAVE
Annual quota: 7 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 3 days.
Minimum notice: 1 day.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval is required.
No carry forward.

MATERNITY LEAVE
Annual quota: 90 days per year.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval and HR approval are required.

PATERNITY LEAVE
Annual quota: 10 days per year.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval is required.

UNPAID LEAVE
Annual quota: 30 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 30 days.
Minimum notice: 7 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval and HR approval are required.
