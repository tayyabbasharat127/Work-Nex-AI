WORKNEX LEAVE POLICY SAMPLE

WHAT IS THIS FILE?
This is a starting template for your organization's leave rules. Replace the
sample numbers below with your own, then upload this file from
Leave Management > Policy Document Automation. WorkNex will read it and show
you the rules it understood, before anything goes live — you approve before
it's activated.

Prefer clicking instead of editing a text file? Use the "Add Manually" button
on the same page instead — same result, no file needed.

HOW TO WRITE A RULE (plain English, no special syntax)
Each leave type is its own block, starting with a heading like "CASUAL LEAVE"
followed by plain sentences. WorkNex looks for phrases like these:

  "Annual quota: 12 days per year."          -> how many days per year
  "Maximum consecutive 3 days."               -> longest single request allowed
  "Minimum notice: 1 day."                    -> how far in advance to apply
  "... up to 1 day are auto-approved ..."     -> short requests approve instantly,
                                                  no manager needed
  "... up to 30% of the team ..."             -> pauses auto-approval if that many
                                                  of the employee's department are
                                                  already on leave the same days
  "Carry forward is allowed up to 5 days."    -> unused days roll into next year
                                                  (up to that cap)
  "No carry forward."                         -> unused days expire at year end
  "Manager approval is required."             -> needs a manager to review
  "Manager approval and HR approval are required." -> needs manager AND admin
  "A medical certificate is required after 2 days." -> sick-leave document rule
  "Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN." -> who this leave type is for
    (supported role names: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN, SUPER_ADMIN)

You don't need every phrase in every block — only include the ones that apply.

Supported leave types: ANNUAL, SICK, CASUAL, MATERNITY, PATERNITY, UNPAID,
BEREAVEMENT, MARRIAGE, STUDY, HAJJ, COMPENSATORY, OTHER.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------

ANNUAL LEAVE
Annual quota: 18 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 10 days.
Minimum notice: 7 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Carry forward is allowed up to 5 days.
Manager approval is required.
Probation employees are not allowed to use annual leave.

SICK LEAVE
Annual quota: 10 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 5 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
A medical certificate is required after 2 days.
Manager approval is required.
No carry forward.

CASUAL LEAVE
Annual quota: 7 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 3 days.
Minimum notice: 1 day.
Casual leave requests up to 1 day are auto-approved without manager sign-off.
Casual leave auto-approval pauses once up to 30% of the employee's department
is already on leave for the same dates — those requests go to a manager instead.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
No carry forward.

MATERNITY LEAVE
Annual quota: 90 days per year.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval and HR approval are required.

PATERNITY LEAVE
Annual quota: 10 days per year.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval is required.

UNPAID LEAVE
Annual quota: 30 days per year.
Maximum consecutive 30 days.
Minimum notice: 7 days.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval and HR approval are required.

HAJJ LEAVE
Annual quota: 40 days per year.
Applicable roles: EMPLOYEE, MANAGER, ADMIN.
Manager approval and HR approval are required.
No carry forward.

Delete any leave type block you don't need, or add your own (e.g. BEREAVEMENT,
MARRIAGE, STUDY, COMPENSATORY) using the same plain-English style as above.
